Intense Repair Keratin Tri-Silk Serum Shampoo

New Dove Intense Repair Shampoo, the damage shampoo and Dove’s best repair shampoo, is upgraded with Keratin Tri-Silk Serum. With Keratin Repair Actives that instantly repairs* and nourishes damaged** hair from the core to the surface. For extra repaired, restored, and soft hair^! *Repairs refers to roughness and dryness **Damage refers to roughness and dryness ^Based on lab test with system vs. non-use. Dove shampoo is a non toxic shampoo just like your favorite organic shampoo.

About this product 

• For damaged* hair
• With Keratin Tri-Silk Serum, a triple action formula with the powerful combination of Keratin, Keratin Repair Actives and Serum Ingredients that instantly repairs damage*, deeply nourishes and protects, to give you that EXTRA beautiful hair
• Helps repair and protects strands to the core

* Damage refers to roughness and dryness.

Ingredients 

Water, Sodium Laureth Sulfate, Dimethiconol, Glycol Distearate, Sodium Chloride, Cocamidopropyl Betaine, Perfume, Glycerin, Sodium Benzoate, Gluconolactone, Citric Acid, Cocamide MEA, TEA-Dodecylbenzenesulfonate, Acrylates/Beheneth-25 Methacrylate Copolymer, Guar Hydroxypropyltrimonium Chloride, TEA-Sulfate, Mica, Sodium Sulfate, Trehalose, PEG-45M, Phenoxyethanol, Disodium EDTA, Titanium Dioxide, Helianthus Annuus (Sunflower) Seed Oil, Lysine HCl, Sodium Lauryl Sulfate, Silica, Hydrolyzed Keratin, Mineral Oil, Tocopheryl Acetate
Ingredients correct at time of publishing. Always check product packaging.

Perfect for 

•For damaged hair
• With Keratin Tri-Silk Serum, a triple action formula with the powerful combination of Keratin, Fiber Actives and Serum Ingredients that instantly repairs damage*, deeply nourishes and protects, to give you that EXTRA beautiful hair.

How to use 

After wetting hair, apply your Dove shampoo and work up a lather, massaging the roots to ensure a deep cleanse. Rinse thoroughly and follow with Dove 1 minute Keratin Repair Serum Conditioner to achieve repaired, restored, and soft hair.
